Overview

About the project

Problem: Graduate students in Georgia Tech often try and get an assistantship positions on campus. Such positions waive of the tuition fees and also provide a monthly stipend, making these positions highly coveted and sought after. However, no formalized system exists in Gatech for grad students to apply for these roles. Hence grad students often rely on tedious and ardous task of bulk cold mailing to professors to try and get a position.

Solution: We designed an app for Georgia Tech graduate students, where they can see all available jobs at one place, apply to these jobs with ease, and even get training for the job they are interersted in applying for.

Timeline: 4 Months

My Role: I was involved in the user research, design of the app, and managing the functioning of the team. I also worked on the evaluation of the final product.

The Challenge

Designing an app for Georgia Tech graduate students that simplifies the process of applying for graduate assistantships, and offers them the opportunity to learn relevant skills along with ease of application to these jobs.

User Research

Identifying the user needs

In our research, we interviewed several students and conducted online surveys to understand the problems being faced by graduate students with assistanship positions, identifying their needs . We wanted answers to questions such as:

  • What are the major challenges faced by graduate students when searching for assistantship positions?
  • How do you come to know if there is a position available or not?
  • How better can this information be conveyed to you?
  • What changes would you like to see in the current system of finding assistantship positions on campus.
